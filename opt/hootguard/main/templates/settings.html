Settings

This page allows you to customize and optimize your HootGuard Sentry setup to suit your needs. From network configurations to Dynamic DNS management and beyond, you’ll find all the tools necessary to tailor your system. Explore the various settings below to enhance security, manage connections, and adjust preferences. Each link will guide you to a dedicated configuration page for a seamless experience.

Adblock - Blacklisting / Whitelisting
If there is a need to fine-tune the Adblocker’s filtering preferences, the options below provide flexibility. Use the whitelist link to ensure specific websites are always accessible, or use the blacklist to block access to certain sites. This approach offers enhanced control over the browsing experience and helps maintain security across all devices connected to the network.
Manage Whitelist
Manage Blacklist
Dynamic DNS Settings
To change your DNS provider or DNS settings, please click the link below. Be aware that if you have active VPN connections, modifying DNS settings may affect the functionality of your VPN clients or connections.

Snooze Settings
To adjust the snooze time, which temporarily disables your HootGuard Adblocker, click the link below. Keep in mind that this deactivation will also apply to all VPN connections, meaning they will no longer use any adblocker during this period.
Snooze Settings
Network Settings
To change the IPv4 or IPv6 address of your HootGuard system, click the link below. Please note that the system will need to reboot after changing the IP address. If you are using VPNs, ensure you update the firewall settings on your ISP router to reflect the new IP address.
