DDNS Settings

To use the VPN feature on your HootGuard Sentry, you can easily set up your own DDNS through Cloudflare or DuckDNS. HootGuard supports IPv4 and IPv6.

Option 1: Configure your Own Cloudflare DDNS

Cloudflare is an easy to setup an free to use DNS service. Please note that you will need your own domain to fully utilize Cloudflare's services. The Cloudflare DNS service supports both IPv4 and IPv6 DDNS in one convenient setup.
Option 2: Configure your Own DuckDNS DDNS

To enhance data privacy, you have the option to enter your own DuckDNS configuration directly here. This approach allows for a customized level of privacy and control. For DuckDNS you don't need your own Domain, you can simply create a DuckDNS subdomain. The DuckDNS service is free to use.
Deactivate Dynamic DNS

If you no longer need your VPN, you can simply deactivate your dynamic DNS by clicking on the button below. This action will disable all DDNS configurations. Note that after deactivation, the VPN function of your HootGuard Sentry will no longer work.
