Initial Setup - Intro

Follow the steps below to easily set up your HootGuard Sentry.

  • Step 1: Set up the network
    Before HootGuard can protect you online, it needs to connect to your local network. If you’ve never done this before, don’t worry—our wizard will suggest a configuration that should work in most homes.
  • Step 2: Configure Dynamic DNS.
    Dynamic DNS (DDNS) keeps a constant address pointing to your Sentry so you can reach your VPN from anywhere. Devices purchased from hootguard.com come with DDNS already in place. If you installed the free edition, enter your preferred DDNS provider here—or skip this step and add it later.
  • Step 3: Create a password
    Choose a strong password to ensure only you can log in to your Sentry. We recommend at least 12 characters mixing upper‑ and lower‑case letters, numbers, and symbols. A memorable pass‑phrase works even better. Avoid reusing passwords from other sites—the Sentry deserves its own unique key.
  • Step 4: Summary and Reboot
    Review all your parameters carefully. Once everything looks correct, confirm your settings to trigger a reboot and apply the changes. The restart usually completes in under two minutes; during this brief window the device and any connected services will be unreachable.
